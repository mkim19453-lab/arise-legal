Arise Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

05 — Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our backend servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your habit data and progress are stored and linked to your account to enable sync across devices and sessions.

We do not store your payment card information. All billing is managed by your app store. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your app store account settings.
